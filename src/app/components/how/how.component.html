A. How It Works


Join a pool of Women In Transport professionals where you share your professional profile; to help you find consultancies/ job offers/ speaking engagements.



Why Showcase your Professional Profile?

We are a women-led Pan-African organization working towards creating safe, sustainable, and accessible public transportation spaces for women and vulnerable groups in Africa by influencing behavioral change, generating knowledge, and movement-building.

The transport sector remains a male-dominated industry. Flone Initiative is determined to attract, retain and advance female professionals in the industry. By having women actively engage in the transport sector, they stand better chances of influencing decision-making on matters of interest to them and other women affected directly or indirectly. The platform shall grant women a platform to showcase their skill set in a more accessible location by potential employers and service providers.

Get Noticed!

You shall be easily noticed whenever there is an opportunity that matches your skills set. The platform offers an excellent opportunity for potential recruiters to access your profile and contact you quickly.

B. Report Fraud

If you notice any form of fraud or harassment, fraud or scam, kindly report to us via communications@floneinitiative.org. We shall delete the candidate/company profile and block them from registering in the future. Therefore, kindly conduct due diligence before committing to any service provider or job seeker.
